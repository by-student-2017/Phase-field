Energy: 1/RT [1/(J/mol)]
Length: 1/b1 = 1/(L/N) = 1/([m]/(number of mesh))
Time: 1/(b1*b1/D), diffusion coefficient D [m2/s]
Time: Ms*RT, Ms [mol/s/J]=[(1/s)*1/(J/mol)], (mobility Mc=Ms=1)
Mc*RT/D [dimensionless]
Concentration gradient energy, kappa: 1/(b1*b1*RT)
